The Vision

Engage, Educate, Empower

As a professional medical illustrator, Christina creates engaging health education visual solutions that enhance the communication between clinicians and their patients. Through a combination of accurate and meaningful illustrations, 3D models, graphics, and interactive learning media she aims to strengthen patients' relationships with their healthcare providers, educate patients, promote shared decision making, and ultimately empower patients to take control of their health.

The Illustrator

Christina Lorenzo

Christina received her Bachelor of Science in Human Physiology from Boston University. After graduating, she worked directly with clinicians and patients in cardiac electrophysiology and nephrology clinical research. Christina is a Master of Science candidate and the Graduate Assistant for the Biomedical Visualization program at the University of Illinois at Chicago.

Currently, Christina is collaborating with Dr. Rasa Kazlauskaite at the Rush University Prevention Center to develop an interactive learning tool to educate patients with type 2 diabetes about continuous glucose monitoring. Additionally, she works as an eLearning Designer Intern at Medline Industries, Inc. helping to build interactive continuing education courses for clinicians.
