The Illustrator

Christina Lorenzo

Christina is a biomedical illustrator who specializes in realism drawing, graphic design, and 3D modeling. She focuses on medical legal illustration, surgical illustration, scientific art, and healthcare education visuals.

Qualifications

Christina received her Bachelor of Science in human physiology from Boston University. After graduating, she worked directly with clinicians and patients in cardiac electrophysiology and nephrology clinical research. Currently, Christina is a Master of Science candidate in the Biomedical Visualization program at the University of Illinois at Chicago.

The Vision

Beauty, Accuracy, Meaning

As a biomedical illustrator, Christina efficiently translates complex medical and biological processes into beautiful, accurate, and meaningful illustrations, 3D models, and interactives.
