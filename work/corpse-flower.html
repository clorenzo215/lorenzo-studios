Flowering Cycle of the Corpse Flower

This educational 3D modeled poster shows the rare flowering cycle of the corpse flower, or Amorphophallus titanum. This giant plant is found in the rainforests of Sumatra, Indonesia, and cycles between 3 to 6 months of dormancy and 12 to 18 months of leaf growth. Occasionally every 7 to 10 years a large bloom will grow instead of a leaf.
